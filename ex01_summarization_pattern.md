## Refine it into a reusable pattern:
**"Summarize the following text in 3 bullet points, focusing on the main arguments and ignoring examples."**

Key regions like the cerebrum (processing, thought), cerebellum (coordination), and brainstem (involuntary life functions) work together to process sensory data, manage memory, and control movement.]


**AI's Output:**
* The human brain is a complex network of billions of neurons and support cells controlling bodily functions, thoughts, emotions, and sensations.
* Key regions like the cerebrum, cerebellum, and brainstem collaborate to process sensory data, manage memory, and control movement.
* These regions work together to govern both voluntary actions and involuntary life functions, ensuring overall brain and body coordination.
